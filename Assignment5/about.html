Why I chose These Website Ideas

Option 1: Local Private Event Rental and Catering Catalog

In my current job I am in charge of booking social events, so I am constantly googling
places that offer catering or private space rentals. There is not a webiste I have found
that has been able to list all local options in one place. That is why one of my ideas is
to create one, so for people in positions like me do not have to click into each restaurant
to try to find out if they have large scale offerings.

Option 2: Photography Portfolio

One of my long standing hobbies is photography. I've taken many family photos, senior
photos, and engagement photos. I've always wanted to put myself out there more, and I
believe a portfolio page would be the perfect way to start. I also think this option
would be the most fun as I would have more design freedom.

Option 3: Corporate Blog

My third option for a website is creating a blog page for my job. I am responsible for
creating a newsletter, and the format we currently use is difficult for people outside
of our organization to see. I believe it would be beneficial to create a seperate
webpage so anyone interested in our program would be able to view what we are doing.
