In The News!

AHEC and FMRWM Faculty and Administration facilitate Indigenous Public Health Summit

AHEC works directly with our residency offering training and continuing education for health professionals.

Dr. Amy Stiffarm presenting at Salish Kootenai College on October 2, 2024. Photo By: Robyn Iron/MTN News

Health care workers from Montana came together to discuss ongoing issues Indigenous communites face in the health care industry. FMRWM Faculty Amy Matheny and American Indian Outreach Coordinator Drew Babcock worked with AHEC to connect health care workers with Dr. Amy Stiffarm to facilitate how to care for Indigenous patients.

"This is just such an incredible opportunity for us to learn and...make a space for those voices for this summit to be Indigenous, led to really help us understand both for those that are here as Indigenous public health and health care practitioners," Amy Matheny said.
