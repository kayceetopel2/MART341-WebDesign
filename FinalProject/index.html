Welcome to Kalispell, Montana

This guide is to give new comers, locals, and people who want to know more about Kalispell an inside look.

5 Things To Do!

  1. Explore Glacier National Park
    Just a short drive from Kalispell, this iconic park offers breathtaking views, hiking trails, and opportunities to spot wildlife. Don't miss the Going-to-the-Sun Road for a scenic drive!
  2. Visit Flathead Lake
    The largest freshwater lake west of the Mississippi, Flathead Lake is perfect for boating, fishing, swimming, or enjoying a peaceful picnic on its shores.
  3. Wander Through Downtown Kalispell
    Discover charming shops, art galleries, and local eateries. Be sure to check out the Conrad Mansion Museum for a glimpse into Kalispell's history.
  4. Hike Lone Pine State Park
    This nearby park features several trails with panoramic views of the Flathead Valley. It's a great spot for a quick hike or picnic.
  5. Experience Local Breweries and Restaurants
    Sample craft beers at local breweries like Tamerack Brewing Company.
